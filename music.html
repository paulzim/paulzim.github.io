Music

Outside of my professional life, I also enjoy playing music. More than anything, I like playing shows. Over my life, I have played (and continue to play) with many bands! Some bands didn’t last too long, but some of my favorite projects are below:

Certain Echos

I played bass in this Isla Vista, California band during my college days. We played mostly covers but had a few originals, and we played parties like it was going out of style.

Mute Angst Envy

I played guitar and keys in Mute Angst Envy, and we had a great run. We were on rotation on Live 105 (San Francisco), even making their top 105.3 songs of 1990! We played some stellar shows, including opening gigs with Xymox and Wolfgang Press.

LuxoCrush

After the demise of MAE, I rejoined with Keith St Thomas to play with LuxoCrush. We still play shows to this day!

Lisa Dewey and the Lotus Life

I joined Lisa Dewey in the early 2000s to play with her full band shows. This is another group that I play with currently!

Fascinating Creatures of the Deep

I played with Fascinating Creatures of the Deep, handling bass for this surf band. We had a great run, with shows up and down the West Coast, Las Vegas, and even Livorno, Italy!
