Leadership

While I enjoy creating things as an individual contributor, I truly found my stride in leadership roles. My first experience as a people manager came in my 20s at Octel Communications, where I learned how to support and guide a team to success. Later at Cisco, I embraced several different leadership roles, each offering unique challenges and opportunities.

No matter the team I manage, I always lead with compassion, a commitment to continuous learning, and a focus on supporting my team. I believe in "leading from the trenches"—working alongside my team to understand their challenges and celebrate their successes.

Program Management

Running programs is another passion of mine. Many of the skills I use as a people manager also apply to successful program management. After earning my PMP certification (cert number 1225518) in 2008, I’ve continually refined my skills in program strategy, execution, and impact measurement.

One of my proudest achievements is leading the team that earned Cisco’s Pioneer Award in 2017 for the innovative DevNet Sandbox. This program empowered developers by providing hands-on access to Cisco’s technology, driving engagement and innovation in the developer community.

Leadership in Action

Beyond my work at the office, I’ve had the privilege of leading initiatives that make a difference in the community. For example, I led a team of Cisco employees in a Habitat for Humanity project in 2017, where we worked together to build homes and strengthen our bond as colleagues and friends.

Leadership is about more than titles—it’s about inspiring others, creating a vision, and fostering an environment where everyone can thrive. I’m excited to continue leading teams, programs, and initiatives that make a positive impact.
